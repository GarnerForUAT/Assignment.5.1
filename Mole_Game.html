The Wackamole Games








Welcome to the wackmole game! Your goal is to score as many points as possible before the game ends!


Click the hole that turns red before it moves to the next hole to get points. Bear in mind, it WILL shuffle around, so be quick!


You have 30 seconds before the game ends. Try to get as many points as you can!
